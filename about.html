About

This website was created by Connor Jensen and Jerica Nicholls as a BYU CS 260 project. In it you will find an array of piano music, along with sheet music and photos of composers that are all available on the public domain under a creative commons license. If you like music, you will almost certainly like this website. If you like music and you don't like this website, then I am concerned for your poor and confused soul.

(Incidentally, this page also has a different background color because one of the TAs doesn't like dark gray backgrounds.)
